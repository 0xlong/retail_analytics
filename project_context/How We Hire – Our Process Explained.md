## What to expect

We want to set everyone up for success, so here’s the lowdown on how we hire. Our process is a two-way street – bringing you into our culture, while helping us learn how you think.

Our full process can last about eight weeks from application to offer, because we care about getting it right. These steps explain how we usually do things.

Before you get started, feel free to consider if you want to work with us. Strange question? Well, we give people a lot of space to navigate their day-to-day and that style isn't for everyone. We want you to be passionate about what you do and be sure this is the right fit. Because when skills and passion combine – it creates that 'Wow' moment.

- ![Three people sit side by side by a large window, each using a laptop or smartphone as one of them laughs.](https://culture.on.com/_next/image/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimages.ctfassets.net%2Fcnz9yoo5vhnk%2F2rjIWOhdK47nQe73RBG0Ww%2F50c49c8f2f5bf40ff66f201827f2c904%2FON2_8750_1.jpg&w=3840&q=75)
	### Step One
	#### It starts with you...
	You'll start by submitting your application to a specific role. We try to keep this step as simple as possible. We do get a lot of applications, but we review them all. If you're a good fit to the role, a recruiter will follow up with you directly. If you didn't receive a reply, or were unsuccessful this time around, we encourage you to look for other possible matches at On.
- ![A person wearing a checked shirt, jeans, and sneakers sits on a wooden bench with a stickered laptop on their lap, gesturing with one hand beneath a white neon wall sign.](https://culture.on.com/_next/image/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimages.ctfassets.net%2Fcnz9yoo5vhnk%2F2skEeHLW91ce8vNBzmzef9%2F6633acd7cacb2bf06dd0064136330854%2FON2_8750_1__1_.jpg&w=3840&q=75)
	### Step Two
	#### Interview with a recruiter
	What ignites your spirit? This is where we’ll start getting to know all about you and what makes you tick – and it’s your first chance to get a feel for our culture. Chatting with a member of our talent team, you’ll learn more about On and how we work. We’ll learn about what motivates you and what you could bring to the team.
- ![A man sits in a glass phone booth on a high swivel chair, working on a laptop while wearing a white T‑shirt, shorts, and white sneakers.](https://culture.on.com/_next/image/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimages.ctfassets.net%2Fcnz9yoo5vhnk%2F1upnLXmya4Ulmyuoj5bmOe%2Fc6f5829663755613bc2ad3bf6ff01358%2FON2_8750_1__2_.jpg&w=3840&q=75)
	### Step Three
	#### Interview with a hiring manager
	Ready to dig into the details? This second interview will be held with your future manager and will focus on the specifics of the job. Together you’ll delve into your unique skills and experiences and how they could be relevant at On. It's also a time to assess how you might feel working side-by-side. Bring any questions you have about the job, the team or anything else you might like to know – this is an open forum.
- ![A person wearing a baseball cap holds open a black-and-white photo book near a window.](https://culture.on.com/_next/image/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimages.ctfassets.net%2Fcnz9yoo5vhnk%2F4gMHVuEXmNmeQ6svNjfd7R%2Ff0e59b6997a860d1b547f0bd747680cb%2FON2_8750_1__3_.jpg&w=3840&q=75)
	### Step Four
	#### The Case Study
	What's your style? This task will help us understand how you think, face a challenge and give insight into your novel ideas. Designed to give everyone their best shot, your case study is based on something you might typically experience on the job. This is your chance to show us what you’ve got. So express yourself. Be you.
- ![Two people walking indoors, smiling and each carrying a laptop—one in a mauve t-shirt and the other in a gray sweatshirt and jeans.](https://culture.on.com/_next/image/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimages.ctfassets.net%2Fcnz9yoo5vhnk%2FyMYj48HyhPTvzCRg2rtzs%2Fc5991bb91c64e75e158e9e63f553626f%2FON2_8750_1__4_.jpg&w=3840&q=75)
	### Step Five
	#### The Experience Day
	Your first taste of the Oniverse. This is a time to meet some of the people you'll be working closest with. In person or virtually, you'll get a feel for the day-to-day at On and the people who make it happen. You'll chat with a few potential teammates - the conversations will be as equally driven by the role and your experience as by our values. We believe how you do things is just as important as what you do.
- ![A smiling woman in a black long-sleeve top and an apron with a "mont-bell" logo looks upward while holding a closed laptop against her chest and a glass of iced beverage.](https://culture.on.com/_next/image/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimages.ctfassets.net%2Fcnz9yoo5vhnk%2F7meG31vHaDd0gmM02lfRNv%2F695a5de21de2f96ef1c4d0e67db52d56%2FON2_8750_1__5_.jpg&w=3840&q=75)
	### Step Six
	#### The Result
	Are we a good fit? We’ll get together internally to share our feedback and decide if we’re a match. We know that job searching is a big time investment, so it’s not a decision we take lightly. It’s also time for you to reflect on the experience – are we the right place for you? No matter the outcome, we’ll give you meaningful feedback.

## Getting the most from your interview

We know they can be stressful.

So, we’re committed to a fair, transparent and inclusive hiring process. We’ve included these tips to help you get the most out of your interview with us.

If you need any extra support or consideration at any time, let us know and we’ll do our best to get it sorted.

![Person seen from behind with long ponytailed hair, wearing a light blue shirt and white pants, holding an open laptop against a blue wall.](https://culture.on.com/_next/image/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimages.ctfassets.net%2Fcnz9yoo5vhnk%2F3koLdrMbI02VGkxV3BASLs%2Fa2f29260e053226373f3f4a06e5db489%2FRectangle_866.jpg&w=1200&q=75)

1\. Do a test run.

Headphones. Internet connection. Microphone. Camera. Make sure your tech is working as it should. If you’re having issues that can’t be fixed, let us know at: start@on-running.com.

We’ll do our best to help, or reschedule your interview if we need to. Not a problem.

2\. Save the date, don't be late.

We’ve got the date, time and interview link confirmed. On the day, hop on the link a few minutes early to make sure everything’s working.

We’ll let you into the chatroom when we’re all set up, but this way you’ll have time to relax before we start.

3\. Get in the zone.

Finding a nice, quiet spot will help you collect your thoughts – and help us hear you better.

If that quiet spot is in your home, feel free to blur your background. We want you to feel as comfortable as possible, so pick the place that suits you best.

4\. Be yourself.

Everyone is different, and that’s how we like it. We want to see the real you. So, wear what makes you comfortable. Ask the questions you want to know.

Grab yourself a water or a coffee to keep you going. And relax – you’ve got this.
